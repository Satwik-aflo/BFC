Recipe Guide

Simple Ways to Use Power-Packed Ingredients

Boring staples, anything-but-boring in the glass. Easy, everyday ways to get turmeric, moringa, and ashwagandha into your routine — no chef skills required.

Lakadong Turmeric

9% curcumin + black pepper — immunity, anti-inflammation, gut support. Typical daily use is 1–2 teaspoons.

Refresher

Turmeric Lemonade

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp Lakadong turmeric powder
  • 4 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 100 ml cold water

Method

Mix turmeric, salt and pepper with the lemon juice thoroughly. Add to a glass of iced water, stir, and enjoy.

Optional: add some carbonated water for an extra refreshing kick.

Morning Ritual

Turmeric Morning Detox

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp Lakadong turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 100 ml lukewarm water

Method

Pour your turmeric and lemon juice into warm water. Mix and sip away for a morning detox.

Boring Foods hack: add ½ tsp moringa to enhance your morning boost.

Moringa

A nutrient-dense leaf — iron absorption, blood-sugar regulation, antioxidant support. Tastes fresh and grassy. Start with ½ teaspoon.

Bestseller · Tastes Like Matcha

Moringa Latte

Ingredients

  • 90 ml cold milk of choice (oat for creaminess)
  • 1 tsp moringa powder
  • Sweetener of choice (fresh strawberries or vanilla essence)

Method

Mix your moringa powder with 2 teaspoons of water to create a concentrate. Add the concentrate to your milk, then add sweetener and froth to combine well.

Boost Your Buttermilk

Moringa Buttermilk

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp moringa powder
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • 5–6 tbsp (80 ml) yogurt
  • A few splashes of water
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Method

Add all ingredients to a blender and mix until well combined. Pour into a glass, add ice, and enjoy.

Add our Karimunda pepper powder for a bold punch.

Breakfast of Champions

Moringa Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp moringa
  • 3–4 spoons Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup milk of choice
  • 3 almonds + 3 walnuts, chopped

Method

Add all ingredients to a bowl, mix till combined. Leave overnight to chill and enjoy.

Easiest Hack

Moringa Rotis & Dosas

Ingredients

  • Your regular atta or dosa batter
  • 2–3 tsp moringa powder (per 4–5 rotis or dosas)

Method

Take your regular atta or dosa batter and add 2–3 teaspoons of moringa powder for 4–5 rotis or dosas. Adjust the amount depending on the quantity of atta.

Boring Foods hack: add our Lakadong turmeric powder for extra immunity.

Ashwagandha

Helps regulate cortisol — stress balance, better sleep, muscle recovery. Earthy and neutral. Start with ½ teaspoon.

Post-Workout · V1

Ashwagandha Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp ashwagandha
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 glass of cold or warm water

Method

Add all ingredients to a glass, mix thoroughly, and enjoy ashwagandha-powered muscle recovery.

Post-Workout · V2

Ashwagandha Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp ashwagandha
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 pack Greek yogurt (100 ml)
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 50 ml oat milk
  • 3 almonds

Method

Blend all ingredients in a mixer, pour into a glass, and enjoy.

A Simple Wind Down

Ashwagandha Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp ashwagandha
  • 1 tsp unsweetened cocoa or drinking chocolate
  • ½ glass warm milk of choice

Method

Add all ingredients to a glass, mix thoroughly, and enjoy a stress-free slumber.

A Simple Wind Down

Ashwagandha Night Tea

Ingredients

  • ½ tsp ashwagandha
  • A mug of any night-time tea you enjoy

Method

To your night-time tea routine, add half a teaspoon of ashwagandha for added sleep benefits. Stir, and enjoy.
